Milo Go

Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2, 2026

Overview

Milo Go is designed with your privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or share any personal information. Your data belongs to you.

Data Collection

Milo Go does not collect any data. Specifically:

  • No personal information is collected
  • No analytics or tracking SDKs are used
  • No advertising frameworks are included
  • No data is sent to the developer or any third party

Location Data

Milo Go uses your device's GPS to record trip routes and distances, including detailed route paths during active tracking. Location data is processed entirely on your device and is never transmitted to external servers. Location tracking only occurs when you start a trip or enable automatic trip detection.

Subscriptions & Purchases

Milo Go offers optional paid subscriptions managed entirely through Apple's App Store. All payment processing is handled by Apple. Milo Go does not collect, store, or have access to your payment information. Subscription status is verified on your device through Apple's StoreKit framework.

Smart Classification

Milo Go learns how you classify trips at specific locations to suggest classifications for future trips. This pattern data is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to any server.

Data Storage

All trip data, settings, and preferences are stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. If you have iCloud enabled, your data may sync across your Apple devices through Apple's CloudKit service. This sync is handled entirely by Apple's infrastructure and is protected by your Apple ID credentials. The developer has no access to your iCloud data.

Third-Party Services

Milo Go does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics platforms, or advertising networks. The only external service used is Apple's iCloud for optional data sync, which is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy.

Data Sharing

Your data is never shared with anyone. Milo Go does not sell, rent, or disclose any user data to third parties.

Data Deletion

You can delete any or all of your trip data at any time within the app. Deleting the app removes all locally stored data. To remove iCloud-synced data, you can manage your iCloud storage through your device's Settings.

Children's Privacy

Milo Go is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect any information from children.

Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy is updated, the changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at support@obesecreature.com.
